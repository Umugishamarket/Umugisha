Amategeko n'Amabwiriza

1. Kwemera Amategeko

Ukoresha UmugishaMarket yemera ko azubahiriza aya mategeko n’amabwiriza yose atangazwa kuri iyi website. Niba utabyemeye, ntukoreshe iyi serivisi.

2. Ubucuruzi

Abacuruzi bemerewe gushyira ibicuruzwa byabo ku rubuga. Buri mucuruzi ashinzwe ukuri n’ubwiza bw’ibicuruzwa bye.

3. Kugura no Kwishyura

Abakiriya basabwa kwishyura ibicuruzwa bahitamo hakurikijwe uburyo bwemewe, harimo Mobile Money cyangwa amakarita y’ifaranga. UmugishaMarket ntushinzwe ibibazo by’amakosa mu kwishyura bikorwa hanze y’urubuga.

4. Kugaruza Ibicuruzwa

Ibicuruzwa bishobora kugarurwa mu gihe byujuje ibisabwa n’amategeko y’igihugu ndetse n’amabwiriza y’UmugishaMarket. Nyamuneka saba amabwiriza yo kugaruza mbere yo gusubiza ibicuruzwa.

5. Ubwirinzi bw'Amakuru

UmugishaMarket yita ku kubungabunga umutekano w'amakuru yawe ariko ntiyemera kuba umunyamabanga ku makuru yawe yose atangwa kuri platform.

6. Guhindura Amategeko

UmugishaMarket ishobora guhindura aya mategeko igihe icyo ari cyo cyose. Impinduka zizatangazwa ku rubuga kandi zizubahirizwa guhera igihe zatangajweho.

7. Guhagarika Serivisi

UmugishaMarket ifite uburenganzira bwo guhagarika serivisi ku muntu wese utubahiriza amategeko cyangwa ugakora ibikorwa bitemewe.
